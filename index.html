Virtual

Suitable for those with a busy schedule who want to study at their own pace, cannot physically attend classes due to distance or other circumstances, and value independence and flexibility in learning.

Hybrid

Suitable for those who want to combine the convenience of online learning with the benefits of in-person classes, value live communication but cannot always attend physically, and seek more freedom in choosing the format of learning depending on the topic or circumstances.

In Person

Suitable for those who better perceive information through live interaction with the instructor, value practical sessions and direct group interaction, and prefer a structured schedule with regular meetings.

Virtual Learning

- Access to lectures and materials 24/7.
- Ability to learn from anywhere with an internet connection.
- Support from instructors via chat, video conferencing, or email.
- Flexibility to fit learning into your schedule and pace.

Hybrid Learning

- In-person sessions for practical exercises and deeper insights into complex topics.
- Online sessions for theory, self-paced work, and reviewing materials at your convenience.
- Modern learning approach with interactive platforms and technologies.
- Flexible schedule combining both formats.

In-Person Learning

- Live classes in an auditorium with instructors and other students.
- Opportunity to participate in group discussions, practical tasks, and workshops.
- Immediate feedback from instructors during lessons.
- Organized learning in a clearly defined time and place.

Learn The Fundamentals

Dive into the essentials of HTML and CSS, the building blocks of web development. This course is perfect for beginners, teaching you how to structure web pages with HTML and style them beautifully with CSS. Gain the foundational skills needed to start creating your own websites!
